Detailed summary

### **1. Title Slide**

* **Title:** Insta Mall — Inventory Management & Local Marketplace
* **Subtitle:** Offline → Online Inventory, Buyer Map Visibility & Growth Platform for Retail Shops (Pakistan)
* **Tagline:** “From Local to Visible — Simple, Affordable, Reliable”

---

### **2. Elevator Pitch**

* A single software handling shop operations, cloud backup, and a buyer marketplace.
* Buyers see only subscribed shops on a map—subscriptions act as marketing.
* Works offline, syncs online when available.
* Affordable (Rs 15k–20k setup) with monthly subscriptions.

---

### **3. Problem Statement**

Shopkeepers face:

1. Manual or spreadsheet inventories → errors and losses.
2. No digital visibility to local buyers.
3. Poor expiry/perishable management.
4. Expensive vendor setups (30–50k).
5. Fear of data loss, lack of training, hidden costs.

---

### **4. Our Solution**

**SmartShop Solution = Product + Marketplace**

* Hybrid system (Offline + Cloud).
* Buyer app on Play Store shows subscribed shops with map routes.
* Customizable UI (50+ themes, logos, brand colors).
* Free training, affordable pricing, easy data recovery.
* Flexible columns, custom reports, and formulas (e.g., Profit %).

---

### **5. Core Product Features (Shop Owner App)**

* Offline-first inventory system (add/edit/delete SKUs).
* Low-stock alerts & reorder suggestions.
* Barcode/QR scanning, product image uploads.
* 50+ UI themes, branded invoices, export to Excel/PDF.
* Expiry tracking & custom formulas (e.g., Profit %).

---

### **6. Cloud & Backup Features**

* Hybrid mode: local DB + daily cloud backup.
* Tiered storage: 1GB / 3GB / 10GB per plan.
* One-click restore, automatic image compression.
* Local export option for .backup files.

---

### **7. Buyer App Features**

* Buyers can search nearby shops by product name.
* Shops listed only if subscribed.
* Shop profiles (logo, hours, contact, offers).
* Reviews, ratings, and distance sorting.
* Map view with polyline routes & Google Maps integration.

---

### **8. Map & Visibility Rule**

* Only subscribed shops appear in Buyer App.
* Visibility hierarchy:

  * **Standard:** listed
  * **Professional:** priority
  * **Full:** featured/top listing
* Promotions visible only to Full plan users.

---

### **9. Subscription Plans**

* Monthly plans for shop owners (Standard, Professional, Full).

---

### **10. What’s Included Where (Feature Matrix)**

* **Cloud Storage:** 1GB / 3GB / 10GB tiers.
* **Data Recovery:** included from Standard; Full = free reinstallation.
* **Visibility:** Standard → listed, Pro → priority, Full → featured.
* **Branding:** scales with plan.
* **Promotions:** Full = unlimited, Pro = 5 products.

---

### **11. Pricing Rationale & Value Proposition**

* Rs 300/month = affordable marketing + cloud backup.
* Branding improves visibility and sales.
* Priority listings drive real-world footfall.
* Pay-as-you-grow avoids high upfront costs.

---

### **12. Onboarding & Support**

* Free YouTube tutorials (Urdu + English).
* Free onsite training (first visit).
* Data recovery included from Standard.

### **13. Competitor Analysis**

**Competitors:**
